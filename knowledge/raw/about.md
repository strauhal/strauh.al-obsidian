https://strauh.al strauh.al is a archive/collection of artworks and images i have downloaded from the internet since 2015/2016.  it is the closest thing to my consciousness that i can capture in html.

the name "strauh.al" comes from my last name and i originally used it as  https://strauh.al/scripts a link to quickly download python scripts .  however, the site grew bigger and eventually there was too much data linked on this website to rename it into something more archival sounding.

if you're on a mobile device and you enjoy using this site, add it to your homescreen.

if you're on desktop and you *really* enjoy using this site, you can create a clone of it on your computer by going to  https://github.com/strauhal/strauh.al3.1 github.com/strauhal/strauh.al3.1 .

strauh.al is continually updated and maintained through donations.  donations can be made through visiting  https://strauh.al/donate strauh.al/donate .

if you have questions or comments about the site, email me at  mailto:ernest@strauh.al ernest@strauh.al .
