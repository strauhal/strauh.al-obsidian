# Alexander Scriabin

**Russian composer who mapped musical keys to colors; an influence behind Ernest's color-as-music experiments.**

## Summary
Listed among the [[influences]]. Scriabin's synesthetic color-organ ambitions resonate
with the [[ideas-sketchbook]] note "color as a temporal axis for the vellum animation,
blending colors," and with [[Wassily Kandinsky]]'s parallel program — both feeding
Ernest's interest in translating one sense into another (see [[Latent Space]]).

## Connections
- [[Wassily Kandinsky]] — fellow synesthete of color and sound.
- [[Latent Space]] — mapping one dimension of sense onto another.
- [[Video Portraiture]] — where "color as a temporal axis" would apply.
