# [strauh.al](https://strauh.al)/dreams

 for this series, i decided to lay on my side while holding a pen above my notebook. i would drift in and out of sleep and my waking reality would become confused with the reality of my dream.

 in the process, the dream would overwrite itself over reality as i would accidentally draw over preexisting sketches and notes while half asleep.
