# Charles Baudelaire

**French poet of *Les Fleurs du mal* and theorist of the flâneur; listed among Ernest's influences.**

## Summary
Listed among the [[influences]]. The flâneur — the wandering urban observer — is a fair
description of the diaristic voice: drifting through Vegas, San Francisco, and Chicago,
cataloguing strangers and signs. (The site's separate *arcades_project* page nods to
Walter Benjamin's Baudelaire studies.)

## Connections
- [[Marcel Proust]] — fellow French literary touchstone.
- [[The Internet as Confidant]] — the observer who reports the city to a reader.
