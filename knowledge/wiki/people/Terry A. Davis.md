# Terry A. Davis

**The late programmer who built TempleOS single-handedly; quoted by Ernest as a prophet of simplicity.**

## Summary
Diary, 2024-10-30, "the late (and great) terry davis": *"An idiot admires complexity, a
genius admires simplicity… they try to make it so complicated people think you're a
genius."* Ernest deploys the quote against artists who mumble "installation" or "arduino"
over a pile of studio trash, and pairs it with Ramanujan — whose results "had already
come to him in dreams" — to argue that "elegance is a virtue."
