# Memory and Preservation

**The drive to capture and keep — memories, images, even rooms and voices — that underwrites the whole archive.**

## Summary
The [[strauh.al Archive]] is itself an act of preservation ("the closest thing to my
consciousness that i can capture in html"). In the diary the impulse turns literal: he
"milks" a café memory of his father (2024-09-24), and on 2024-12-25 tries "to do a neural
radiance field of my grandmas house so that I can hold onto this memory forever." It
shades into dread, too — the inherited compulsions of his stock-obsessed grandfather
(2024-12-29), behaviors "repeating… but with faster tools."

## Key points
- Technology as memory prosthesis: NeRF, the archive, the diary, the [[kits.ai Voice]].
- The Proustian detail (see [[Marcel Proust]]) as the seed of recollection.
- In tension with [[Autofiction]] — what's preserved is already narrativized.
