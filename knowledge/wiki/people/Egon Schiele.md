# Egon Schiele

**Austrian expressionist draughtsman of raw, contorted figures; an influence Ernest wants to fuse with others.**

## Summary
Listed among the [[influences]]. The [[ideas-sketchbook]] records a working note —
"combining paul jenkins with egon schiele and ilya repin" — placing Schiele in Ernest's
recipe for a personal figurative style, prized for the nervous immediacy of his line.

## Connections
- [[Spontaneity and Elegance]] — the immediacy of the drawn line.
- [[Video Portraiture]] — Ernest's own figurative, line-based practice.
