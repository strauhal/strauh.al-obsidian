# Implicit Distance

**A 2023 audiovisual composition (EP) built from warped synthesizer sketches and a StyleGAN trained on images from Ernest's own life.**

## Summary
Ernest arrived at the piece's sonic aesthetic by overlaying synthesizer sketches and
running them through warping algorithms: when an audio clip is stretched to its limit,
the algorithm fills the gap by *interpolating between each audio sample*. The
accompanying visuals were generated with StyleGAN interpolating through a personal
image dataset. The title names the work's core idea — distance made audible and
visible through interpolation — which is why it doubles as a recurring concept in his
thinking (see [[Latent Space]]).

## Key points
- Sound: synth sketches → warping/time-stretch → sample-level interpolation.
- Visuals: StyleGAN walk through a dataset "from my life" — a [[Latent Space]] of self.
- Streamable as the *Implicit Distance EP* (Apple Music) and on YouTube.
- The same interpolation logic recurs in the [[ideas-sketchbook]] (e.g. "color as a
  temporal axis," "implicit distance layers").
