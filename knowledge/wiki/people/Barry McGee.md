# Barry McGee

**San Francisco graffiti artist and painter; an influence whose framing of train tags as proto-internet stuck with Ernest.**

## Summary
Listed among the [[influences]]. Diary, 2024-12-22: "in a lecture i attended, barry
mcgee described traincar tags as 'the internet before the internet.' i like that." The
line ties McGee directly to two of Ernest's recurring ideas — [[Trainpilled]] and
[[The Internet as Confidant]] — networks of messages moving across the country, signed
and anonymous at once.

## Connections
- [[Trainpilled]] — train culture as a message network.
- [[The Internet as Confidant]] — tags and the net as kindred systems.
