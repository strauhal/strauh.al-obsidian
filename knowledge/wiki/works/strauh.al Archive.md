# strauh.al Archive

**The website itself: an archive of artworks and images Ernest has downloaded since 2015–2016 — "the closest thing to my consciousness that i can capture in html."**

## Summary
The name comes from his surname; it began as a quick link for downloading Python
scripts and grew until there was too much data to rename it into "something more
archival sounding." It is organized by decade (900s–2000s) and by theme (ukiyo-e,
comics, portraits, wallpapers, album covers, and more), and is mirrored on GitHub so
anyone can clone it. Maintained through donations.

## Key points
- Self-description: a captured *consciousness* in HTML — the through-line for
  [[The Internet as Confidant]] and [[Memory and Preservation]].
- The [[diary]] is itself published as part of the site, in raw HTML "for others to
  read if they have the link."
- The [[ideas-sketchbook]] is the site's running notebook of unbuilt features and aphorisms.
