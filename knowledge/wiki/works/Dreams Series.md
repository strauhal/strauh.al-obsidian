# Dreams Series

**A series of ~43 drawings made on the threshold of sleep, where dream and waking reality overwrite each other on the page.**

## Summary
For this series Ernest lay on his side holding a pen above a notebook, drifting in and
out of sleep. As he dozed, "the dream would overwrite itself over reality" — he would
accidentally draw over preexisting sketches and notes while half asleep, so each sheet
records the collision of the two states.

## Key points
- Method *is* the meaning: no separation between intention and mark — a pure case of
  [[Spontaneity and Elegance]] ("the smallest time lag between the ache to draw and its expression").
- Hosted as image scans (dream_00000–dream_00042) in the strauh.al archive.
- Resonates with the diary's preoccupation with dreams, VR dreams, and altered states.
