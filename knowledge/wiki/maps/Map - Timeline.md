# Map - Timeline

**The vault read as a sequence — the life and the work on one axis.** The atlas is strong on *what connects to what* and weak on *what came before what*; this is the spine that fixes time. Life events sit beside the work they produced. Dates are from [[Ernest Strauhal|Ernest]]'s own account, the [[diary]], and the work notes.

## 2000–2014 · before the archive
- **2000** — born Ernest Charles Strauhal · 許志仁, 8:30am, October 15, Children's Hospital, [[san francisco|San Francisco]]. The youngest and oldest of four. ([[Ernest Strauhal]] · [[Family]])
- **2003** — the family moves from San Francisco to Sacramento.
- **2004** — Elliot Ranch Elementary.
- **2005** — wins a citywide art competition.
- **2008** — starts piano lessons (the instrument he will return to, teaching, in 2026).
- **2012 / 2014** — Elizabeth Pinkerton Middle School, then West Campus High.

## 2015–2019 · the archive and the first exit
- **2015–2016** — [[strauh.al Archive]] begins: images saved "since 2015–2016," "the closest thing to my consciousness that i can capture in html."
- **2018** — accepted into the design program at CCA (formerly CCAC).
- **2019** — drops out of design school — the first entry in [[Failures and Abandoned Projects]].

## 2020–2023 · school again, the slop, the turn
- **2020–2025** — the [[ideas sketchbook 2020 25|sketchbook]] runs, seeding [[The Tedium of the Art Is the Goal]], [[Transmitting My Neural Signals by Hand]], [[Interpolating the Instruction Set]].
- **2022** — accepted into graduate school at SAIC. Also the "slop period" (2022–early 2023) and **Untitled 4**, the line drawing behind the [[GAN Color Studies]].
- **2023** — gallery shows in New York and Chicago; music performances and releases around Chicago. [[Ryuichi Sakamoto]] dies (March) and "re-seriousizes" the music. [[Implicit Distance]] · [[GAN Color Studies]] · [[Untitled 15 - Dynamic Color Study 1]].

## 2024 · the collapse
- Unable to find work in Chicago, he moves into his aunt and uncle's basement in suburban Illinois, dependent on antidepressants — the [[diary]] project begins here.
- [[Vocal Study 3]] · [[Arcades Project (Revisited)]].
- **2024-09-13** — [[The Record Label]] abandoned, diagnosed against [[The Attention Economy]].
- **2024-10-24** — [[Video Portraiture]] named as the current practice. The MIT architecture application goes out (see [[Failures and Abandoned Projects]]).

## 2025 · the bottom, and redemption
- Failure in Chicago; the return to California; suicidal ideation; and the turn — redemption, and a job teaching visual art to children on digital whiteboards (projections and touchscreens).
- **2025-02-13** — [[SEEKER]]. **2025-03-01** — [[kits.ai Voice]] surfaces (an Arpeggi Labs deposit).
- Portrait of [[Megan]]; [[draw for me|computerdrawing.club]] launches; audience correspondence begins.

## 2026 · the present
- Proposes to [[Megan]]. Takes a music-conservatory job — playing and teaching piano to mentally and physically handicapped children and adults, closing the loop back to the 2008 lessons and the family [[Ernest Strauhal|teaching lineage]].
- **2026-06** — this vault is compiled. The [[Map - Recent Dreams|seven dreams]] are recorded.
- [[The Ceremony]] — forthcoming; the line the dreams were logged *before*.
