# Balthus

**Modern figurative painter whose work gave Ernest an instinctive unease that later found its explanation.**

## Summary
Diary, 2024-11-12: Ernest got a free Balthus monograph from his school library and "some
things just felt... off about his paintings"; his father, flipping through it, "also told
me that he got a strange feeling." He left the book at the bottom of a stairwell, and
later found "decade old articles calling balthus a pedophile. hm. interesting how art can
say so much without saying a word at all." The entry is really about trusting an aesthetic
gut-reaction before you can name it.
