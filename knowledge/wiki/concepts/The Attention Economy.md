# The Attention Economy

**The endless, personalized feed — Ernest's recurring antagonist, blamed for hollowing out art, activism, and his own focus.**

## Summary
He wants TikTok, Reels, and Shorts banned outright: "millions (perhaps billions) of
man-hours will be taken back" (2024-09-13) — and admits the feed is why he abandoned
[[The Record Label]]: "how can artists/musicians compete with an endless stream of
entertainment that is tailored to the user?" His [[Andy Warhol]] update names it as the
last shared American experience: "everyone (rich and poor) doomscrolls on the same apps
on the same phones." Social-media activism becomes "a race to the bottom" (see [[AI Slop]]),
and even his own focus is collateral: "any frustration in my mind can be numbed with an
endless feed."
