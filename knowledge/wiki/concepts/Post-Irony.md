# Post-Irony

**The unstable register Ernest writes and lives in — where sincere, ironic, and post-ironic can no longer be told apart, even by him.**

## Summary
Annotating an angry early entry months later (2024-09-01 → 09-07): "i think i was trying
to be really angsty when i wrote this. i can't remember if this was meant to be sincere,
ironic, or post-ironic." He crowns "rebecca black… the queen of post irony" (2024-10-27)
and signs off a recurring credo: "**if you're not being cringe, you're not being based**"
(2025-01-30). The mode is both a defense (covering hurt "with memes and ironic language")
and the thing he keeps trying to write his way out of.
