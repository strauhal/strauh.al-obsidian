# Playboi Carti

**American rapper; listed among Ernest's influences and paired, surprisingly, with the abstract expressionist [[Clyfford Still]].**

## Summary
Diary, 2024-09-14: "thinking about playboi carti and clyfford still today. they are very
similar to each other." The pairing is pure Ernest — collapsing the distance between a
rage-energy rapper and a monumental abstract painter, an instance of his habit of
finding hidden adjacencies (cf. [[Latent Space]]).

## Connections
- [[Clyfford Still]] — the unexpected twin.
- [[Latent Space]] — the impulse to map far-apart things as near.
