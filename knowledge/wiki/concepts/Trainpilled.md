# Trainpilled

**Ernest's self-described affinity for trains and rail — and, by extension, a whole aesthetic of slow, analog, distinctly-American infrastructure.**

## Summary
"i normally consider myself a very trainpilled individual" (2024-09-16), riding the
Metra often (2024-09-03) and watching the Amish pass through the station. The motif peaks
when [[Barry McGee]] describes traincar graffiti tags as "the internet before the
internet" (2024-12-22) — "i like that" — linking rail culture directly to
[[The Internet as Confidant]]. It sits among his other Americana fixations: the shopping cart "monument"
in a field (2024-10-17), war memorials, the I-5.

## Connections
- [[Family]] — the ancestral root: a maternal great-(great-)grandfather helped build the California railroads. The affinity was in the blood before it was an aesthetic.
- [[Barry McGee]] — traincar tags as a pre-internet network.
- [[The Internet as Confidant]] — graffiti and the net as kindred message systems.
