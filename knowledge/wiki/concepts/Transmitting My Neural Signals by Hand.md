# Transmitting My Neural Signals by Hand

**A seed from the [[ideas sketchbook 2020 25|sketchbook]]: drawing as the direct, lossy transmission of an inner state — the hand as the wire out of the brain.**

## Summary
The sketchbook pairs "transmitting my neural signals by hand" with "psychological
notation" — the idea that a mark is not a picture of a feeling but a *recording* of the
nervous system that made it. It is the hand-made counterpart to [[Latent Space]]: where
a model encodes the self as coordinates a machine can sample, the drawn line encodes the
self as a gesture only the body could have produced. [[Video Portraiture]] sits exactly
on this seam — a human hand re-transmitting a captured face, frame by frame — and
[[kits.ai Voice]] is its uncanny inverse, the signal transmitted *without* the hand.

## Key points
- The mark as EEG: the trace carries the state, not just the subject.
- "Psychological notation" — a score for the inner life, kin to synesthetic mapping.
- The hand is the lossy, irreplaceable channel; the loss is the signature.

## Connections
- [[Video Portraiture]] — the practice that most literally re-transmits by hand.
- [[Latent Space]] — the machine encoding of the same self; this is its bodily twin.
- [[kits.ai Voice]] — transmission *without* the hand; the uncanny opposite.
- [[Dead Architecture]] — motion without a body that performed it.
- [[The Tedium of the Art Is the Goal]] — the labor the hand pays.
- [[Alexander Scriabin]] / [[Wassily Kandinsky]] — older dreams of notating one sense as another.
