# Piero Scaruffi

**Italian-American critic notorious for encyclopedic, severe music ratings; a figure Ernest jokingly fears becoming.**

things up to interpretation": "i'm slowly turning into scaruffi." A wry self-diagnosis of
his cataloguing, ranking, completist instinct — the same drive behind the
[[strauh.al Archive]] itself.
