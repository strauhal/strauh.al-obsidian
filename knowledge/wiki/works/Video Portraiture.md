# Video Portraiture

**Ernest's current art practice (named in the diary, 2024-10-24): video captures of people's faces, with frames traced onto vellum.**

## Summary
"It's really not that complex, I'm just doing video captures of people's faces, and
tracing various frames on vellum." He half-jokes that he can "milk this technique for
all it's worth for the next few years." A friend's paper (referenced 2025-01-28)
describes the loop: the neural network makes aesthetic choices, but his hand has the
final say — he integrates the network's output back into the drawing and color-corrects
in Photoshop. He later doubts even that, worried the image is "still funneled through a
series of algorithms well outside of my control" (see [[AI Slop]]).

## Key points
- Hand + machine loop: trace → neural net → re-integrate by hand → correct.
- The [[ideas-sketchbook]] tracks the technical wishlist: "color as a temporal axis for
  the vellum animation," "turn vellumswing into live," "a real time neural typography."
- Continuous with [[Implicit Distance]]'s interpolation logic and the [[Dreams Series]]' tracing.
