# kits.ai Voice

**Ernest's voice, published as a public AI voice model on kits.ai (Arpeggi Labs) — he is paid micro-royalties whenever someone uses it.**

## Summary
Diary, 2025-03-01: he notices a deposit from Arpeggi Labs in San Francisco and realizes
it's from kits.ai, where his voice is publicly available. "It's cool that I'm getting
paid… but then I thought about how often and how many people are using my voice to the
point where I'm making $10 just by doing nothing. **my voice is now everyone's voice.**"
A literal, lived instance of [[AI Slop]] and [[The Oedipal Screen]] anxieties about
losing control of one's own image/output to the machine.
