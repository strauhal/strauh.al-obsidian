dreams

dream 1
naked outside a target but there’s a bunch of random trash on the table in front of me like medication bottles, alcohol bottles, etc.  i think i see a shy coworker from an old job, the one with bleached hair and freckles. trying to work on a job application for palantir but the only thing i have on is a bath towel and the questions are really weird and they’re asking me about obscure musicians and critical theorists but there’s a dozen windows open that have a strange contemporary graphic design language, you know the one where they go crazy with the fonts.  i eventually have to stand in front of a panel of these people and answer all their questions verbally.  i’m so congested that i sound like alan rickman in the harry potter movies and i point it out


dream 2
another dream:  some conventionally attractive guy and his two younger brothers are in my parent’s dining room.  i keep on running across the street since i’m debating whether i should step out and get a drink at the clubhouse across the street.  when i am eating dinner, a large portion of my jacket falls into my soup bowl and is soiled.  i am running around the lobby of the clubhouse looking for a place to leave my jacket for their laundry service.  i open a large paper bag to put my jacket in on the couch and there is a cat inside that meows at me.  there is a whole sequence that unfolds outside like it’s a combination of the devil wears prada and a marvel movie.  i don’t like either genre of those movies.  i step back in and one of the younger boys turned into asmongold and he’s hooked into a laptop with vr glasses on and he’s just talking into the air in front of my mother.  i don’t like twitch streamers in general and i’ve only been exposed to clips of his on twitter.  an old employer of mine who had a stroke recently keeps calling me and mumbling nonsense in a low gravelly voice


dream 3
was at a conference room that vaguely resembled an old church i didn’t like going to as  child, it was a chinese community church.   they were announcing awards for artworks and i was kind of zoned out since i wasn’t expecting to get one.  they called me up for third place and they were going on about the strauh.al archive and how great it was.  when they loaded up a live demo on the projector, it didn’t quite work.  strauh.al loads images instantly when you mouse over an image hyperlink but in my dream, it didn’t work.  instead, there was just one scene that you couldn’t change and it was slightly 3 dimensional compared to the 2d images on the site.  mind you this is happening on a huge projector in front of everyone. anyway, they announced love that the website wasn’t working so i hurried home in my dads beetle to fix the bug.  i drive past a plaza and i see a bunch of families looking people.  my childhood friend jeremy is graduating.  i see some other people and add them all to a group chat.   one of the people in the group chat is paranoid about surveillance and gets mad at me., i stop at a convenience store (i think it was a trader joe’s) and i see the panelist / judge there.  he’s a nice korean guy that organizes board game nights for the community in sacramento at bars/wineries.  i fix the bug there and before i head out, i dictate into my phone that i fixed the bug and i’m going to head home to push the update on github.  in front of him.  i realized what i just did and jokingly told him that i’m responsible for atomization


dream 4
i am driving drunk around my hometown in a suburb of sacramento. california and they have police officers at every other stoplight and onramp.  on my mind:  someone made a 4chan post about the woman i just married and it has the typical 4chan misogyny in the comments.  i have to drive home quickly to reply on my computer.  someone speculates that we’re dating based on people we are connected to online.  one user adds a link to a low resolution youtube video where a musician is talking to her.  another user remarks that i need to be kept away from her and says something about how all my creativity will disappear if i am attached to her.  

note: i had this dream around midnight and at 1:00 i was reading a section of man and his symbols by jung where a man is having a similar predicament :

Several patients have told me dreams in which the motif of sacrifice is combined with the motif of the sacred marriage. One of these was produced by a young man who had fallen in love but was unwilling to marry for fear that marriage would become a kind of prison presided over by a powerful mother figure. His own mother had been a strong influence in his childhood, and his future mother-in-law presented a similar threat.
Would not his wife-to-be dominate him in the same way these mothers had dominated their children?

after a dream he had, this happened:

This dream brought home to my patient the fact that he was more than ready to change his attitude to life. He had been self-centered, seeking the illusory safety of personal independence but inwardly dominated by the fears caused by childhood subjection to his mother. He needed a challenge to his manhood in order to see that unless he sacrificed his childish state of mind he would be left isolated and ashamed. The dream, and his subsequent insight into its meaning, dispelled his doubts. He had passed through the symbolic rite by which a young man gives up his exclusive autonomy and accepts his shared life in a related, not just heroic, form.
And so he married and found appropriate fulfillment in his relationship with his wife. Far from impairing his effectiveness in the world, his marriage actually enhanced it

teaching at one of my non verbal student’s homes today.  i am running late but for some reason i decided to grab food.  i…  venmo someone to go inside for me and order?  very strange.  i’m not the type of person to do something like that. i don’t have venmo installed so i have to go download it from the app store while they’re inside.  when they finally get my food, they get in their car and try to drive off. i am gripping their windshield wipers as they speed through traffic
