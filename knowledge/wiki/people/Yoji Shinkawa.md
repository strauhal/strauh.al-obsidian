# Yoji Shinkawa

**Japanese illustrator and lead artist of the *Metal Gear* series, famed for gestural ink brushwork; listed among Ernest's influences.**

## Summary
Listed among the [[influences]]. Shinkawa's loose, confident ink line — designs that read
as fast brush gestures rather than rendered illustration — is squarely in Ernest's
[[Spontaneity and Elegance]] lane, alongside [[Egon Schiele]].

## Connections
- [[Spontaneity and Elegance]] — the gestural, immediate line.
- [[Egon Schiele]] — kindred draughtsman.
