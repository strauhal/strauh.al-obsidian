# Dream 4 - Marriage, 4chan, and the Flight Home

## Dream Record

Ernest drives drunk through a heavily policed version of his hometown while thinking about a misogynistic 4chan thread concerning the woman he has just married. Commenters claim marriage will extinguish his creativity. A Jung passage read shortly afterward offers a striking counter-image: shared life as the sacrifice of isolated autonomy rather than the destruction of efficacy.
