# The Spatial Web

**Knowledge as a place you move through — the wish to navigate information in three dimensions instead of scrolling a list.**

## Summary
The [[ideas sketchbook 2020 25|sketchbook]] keeps circling it: "spatial z index for the
spatial web," "3d atlas," "hegel3datlas," and "for the 3d atlas, have an option that
rearranges the words based on significance." It is the interface-level expression of
[[Latent Space]] — if the self and its ideas have coordinates, you should be able to *walk*
them. The wish is now literally built: [[Map - 3D Graph]] is a 3D atlas of this vault, and
[[SEEKER]] spatializes the image corpus. This concept is the bridge from the idea to the
tools that realize it.

## Key points
- Navigation over enumeration — depth and adjacency instead of the scroll.
- "Rearrange by significance" — the layout itself carries meaning (exactly the 3D graph's design).
- The self-referential node: the vault you're reading is an instance of it.

## Connections

- [[SEEKER]] — spatial access to the image archive.
- [[Interpolating the Instruction Set]] — movement through generative space.
- [[strauh.al Archive]] — the corpus being spatialized.
