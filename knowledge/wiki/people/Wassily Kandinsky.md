# Wassily Kandinsky

**Pioneer of abstraction who heard color as music; listed among Ernest's influences.**

## Summary
Listed among the [[influences]]. Kandinsky's conviction that painting could work like
music — pure form and color as emotional sound — anchors Ernest's synesthetic streak,
shared with [[Alexander Scriabin]] and realized technically in the audiovisual
[[Implicit Distance]].

## Connections
- [[Alexander Scriabin]] — the musical-color counterpart.
- [[Implicit Distance]] — sound and image generated together.
- [[Latent Space]] — sense translated across dimensions.
