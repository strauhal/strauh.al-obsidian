# Ryuichi Sakamoto

**Japanese composer and electronic-music pioneer; a stated influence whose death marked a turning point in Ernest's relationship to his own music.**

## Summary
Listed among Ernest's [[influences]]. In the diary (2024-09-08) he calls 2022–early-2023
his "slop period" — stretching 15-second snippets into 6-minute pieces — and says "it
wasn't until ryuichi sakamoto died that i realized i needed to put more effort into my
music." Sakamoto thus sits at the hinge between the abandoned [[The Record Label]] era
and the more serious work of [[Implicit Distance]].

## Connections
- [[Implicit Distance]] — the more deliberate music that followed this reckoning.
- [[The Record Label]] — the "slop period" project he left behind.
- [[Tim Hecker]] — another electronic/ambient touchstone.
