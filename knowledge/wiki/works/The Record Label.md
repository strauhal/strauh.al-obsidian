# The Record Label

**An abandoned attempt to start a record label — Ernest got as far as a webpage and Bandcamp page before giving up.**

## Summary
In the diary (2024-09-13) he diagnoses why he quit: "how can artists/musicians compete
with an endless stream of entertainment that is tailored to the user?" He realized this
about [[The Attention Economy]] only after starting — TikTok had already been around for
years. The label stands as a small case study of his broader argument that the feed has
hollowed out the audience for sustained creative work.

## Connections
- [[The Attention Economy]] — the stated reason for abandoning it.
- [[Ryuichi Sakamoto]] — Sakamoto's death is what re-seriousized his music around the same period.
