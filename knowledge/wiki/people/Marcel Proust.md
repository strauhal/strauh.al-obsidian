# Marcel Proust

**Author of *In Search of Lost Time*; listed among Ernest's influences, and the patron of his preoccupation with involuntary memory.**

## Summary
Listed among the [[influences]]. Though the diary never names him, Proust's signature
move — a memory milked from a single sensory detail — is everywhere in Ernest's writing:
"milking" a café memory of his dad (2024-09-24), the NeRF scan of his grandma's house to
"hold onto this memory forever" (2024-12-25). See [[Memory and Preservation]].

## Connections
- [[Memory and Preservation]] — the Proustian impulse to keep the past.
- [[Charles Baudelaire]] — fellow French literary touchstone.
