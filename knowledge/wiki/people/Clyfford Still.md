# Clyfford Still

**American abstract expressionist painter, known for jagged fields of color; in Ernest's mind, a twin to [[Playboi Carti]].**

## Summary
Appears in the diary (2024-09-14) as one half of the "they are very similar to each
other" pairing with [[Playboi Carti]] — both, perhaps, all raw force and torn edges.

## Connections
- [[Playboi Carti]] — the pairing.
- [[Spontaneity and Elegance]] — the appeal of raw, unsmoothed force.
